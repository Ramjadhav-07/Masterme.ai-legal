Delete Account

Effective Date: January 2026

If you wish to permanently delete your MasterMe.ai account and associated learning data, you may request account deletion using the instructions below.

How To Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account, please send an email to:

support@masterme.ai

Include The Following Information

  • Registered email address
  • Registered phone number
  • Student or school name (if applicable)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Happens After Deletion

Once your deletion request is verified and processed:

  • Your account will be permanently removed
  • Quiz history and learning progress may be deleted
  • Access to school dashboards may be revoked
  • You may lose access to purchased subscriptions or premium features

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within:

7 working days

Data Retention

Certain information may be retained temporarily where required for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security purposes
  • Financial transaction records

Need Help?

If you need assistance regarding account deletion or privacy concerns, contact us at:

support@masterme.ai
